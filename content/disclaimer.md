# Disclaimer

*Last updated: June 12, 2026*

## AI Reconstructions — Not the Real Person

Every conversation on Legends Library is an **AI reconstruction**, not the real person. The figures you talk to are simulated personas generated by artificial intelligence and grounded in each figure's primary sources — their letters, speeches, books, interviews, and other documented writings. They are not the actual individual, their estate, or anyone authorized to speak for them, and nothing they say should be attributed to the real person as a new statement.

- Responses are constructed from the historical record, but they are interpretations, not channeling. The real person never said the new sentences our system generates.
- Every response carries a confidence label. **Documented** means it rests on direct evidence; **inferred** means it extends from evidence without being explicit; where there is no honest basis, the figure refuses to answer.
- Figures will not claim knowledge of events that postdate their documented record, and simulated figures may decline questions the historical record cannot support.
- Historians, theologians, and scholars may reasonably disagree with any reconstruction. Treat answers as well-sourced perspective, not historical authority.

## Fair-Use Quoting Policy

Legends Library is built to respect the rights of authors, publishers, and estates.

- Direct quotations from copyrighted sources are limited to **brief excerpts of no more than 300 characters**, used for commentary, scholarship, and education.
- Each figure operates under a **per-source quote budget** that caps how much of any single work can ever be quoted across responses, keeping total usage well within fair-use bounds.
- Quotations are always attributed to their source. Where a source cannot be quoted within these limits, the system paraphrases and cites instead.
- If you are a rights holder and believe material on Legends Library exceeds fair use, contact us and we will review and, where appropriate, remove or restrict the material.

## No-Likeness Video Rule

Legends Library does **not** create synthetic video or voice likenesses of real people.

- We do not generate deepfake video, cloned voices, or any audiovisual impersonation of any real person, living or dead.
- Conversations are text only. Portraits used on the site are illustrative artwork, not implied endorsements.
- Any debate or "council" feature is clearly presented as a simulated, text-based reconstruction.

## No Professional Advice

Content on Legends Library is for educational and entertainment purposes only. It is not legal, medical, financial, religious, or any other form of professional advice, and it does not represent the views of Legends Library, its team, or any real person depicted.
